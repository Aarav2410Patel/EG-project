Overview

The Autonomous Funnel-Dispenser Rover is a custom-built 4-wheel robotic platform designed for wireless operation and material dispensing applications. This DIY project combines Arduino/ESP microcontroller technology with mechanical design to create a versatile mobile platform. The integrated funnel mechanism allows for controlled dispensing of materials while maintaining full mobility through its independent motor-driven wheels.

Hardware Components

Microcontroller & Control

  • ESP32 or separate Wi-Fi Module
  • L298N Motor Driver Module (2 units)
  • Wireless Communication Module
  • Breadboard for connections

Power & Motors

  • 4-6 DC Geared Drive Motors
  • Seed Dispenser Motor
  • Battery Pack and Regulator (12V)
  • Power Distribution Circuit
  • Voltage Regulation System

Mechanical Components

  • Chassis (Base Platform)
  • Wheels/Tracks (Yellow)
  • Seed Hopper (Funnel)
  • Seed Dispenser Plate
  • Connecting Wires & Tracking Wires
  • Jumpers & Header Pins

Sensors & Accessories

  • IMU Sensor (MPU-6050)
  • Encoders for precise movement
  • Moisture Sensor
  • Small Tine or Fixed Furrow Opener
